# Getting Started with Charts - Reports and Graphs for Jira Dashboard

Welcome to **Charts - Reports and Graphs for Jira Dashboard**! This guide will help you get started with creating interactive, customizable charts and tables from your Jira data.

![Charts for Jira Dashboard Banner](https://marketplace.atlassian.com/product-listing/files/ff2d5984-9ef2-453f-8fd2-dd74ca44d571?width=1840&height=900)

---

## Key Features

- **Interactive Charts & Table Views**  
  Visualize Jira issues with **bar, line, pie, area, and scatter charts**, plus dynamic table views for a clear and organized display of your data.

- **Advanced Customization & Branding**  
  Personalize charts with your **logo, name, and colors**. Adjust **chart type, segment order, axis labels, and color opacity** for a professional and unique look.

- **Flexible Data Selection**  
  Choose **projects, fields, or labels** as your data sources to focus on the information that matters most.

- **Grouping & Analysis Options**  
  Group data by **status, assignee, issue type, project, or labels**. Select your analysis mode: **project comparison** or **data aggregation** to gain deeper insights.

- **Enhanced Dashboard Insights**  
  Build dashboards that are **interactive, visually engaging, and easy to explore**, providing actionable insights in real time.

---

## Getting Started

1. **Add the App to Your Jira Dashboard**  
   Install the app and open it from your Jira dashboard.

2. **Select Your Data Sources**  
   Choose the projects, fields, or labels you want to include in your charts.

3. **Choose Chart Type**  
   Select from **bar, line, pie, area, or scatter charts** depending on your data and visualization preference.

4. **Customize Your Charts**
   - Add your **logo, name, and colors**.
   - Reorder segments, hide axis labels, or adjust color opacity.
   - Switch between chart types anytime.

5. **Group and Analyze Data**  
   Group by **status, assignee, issue type, project, or labels**.  
   Pick your analysis mode: **project comparison** or **data aggregation**.

6. **Add Charts to Dashboard**  
   Once configured, add charts to your Jira dashboard for **interactive, real-time insights**.
